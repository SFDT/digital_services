Come Build a Digital City

We’re Hiring Technologists

We're hiring. Check out the open roles below, and fill out the form at the bottom of the page if you're interested.

The San Francisco Digital Services Team is transforming how residents interact with the City. We're doing this by building services designed around the people that use them.

Led by the Chief Digital Services Officer, our team includes:

  • product managers
  • designers
  • business analysts
  • developers
  • content strategists

We believe in four principles:

  1. Put the needs of residents first
  2. Focus on delivery and outcomes over process
  3. Create an agile and data driven culture
  4. Make City services accessible to everyone.

We have some exciting and high-profile products in our pipeline. We're re-building the city’s website from the ground up. We're also working on affordable housing applications and permitting new cannabis businesses.

In these roles, you'll help change culture and practices. You'll need to roll your sleeves to work on the development, operation, and improvement of complex digital services. As part of this new team, you’d have an opportunity to shape the City’s approach to digital services.

The Roles

Product Manager

We are looking for an expert product manager to lead multi-disciplinary development of new user-focused digital services. While the products you work on may vary, our team is currently working on services that help people find affordable housing, start a business, find work, register as a cannabis business, find a pro-bono lawyer for immigration help, and more. As a Digital Services PM you’ll help manage stakeholders and the product team around common goals, manage delivery using disciplined agile methodology, and ensure that analytics and feedback are used to improve the product. You’ll also be a champion for user needs, improving business processes, and cutting bureaucratic red tape. Successful candidates will have leadership experience on agile product teams.

The Housing Service Team

We’re growing the digital services team that operates and iteratively improves the affordable housing service (housing.sfgov.org), as well as the Salesforce implementation used to manage larger affordable housing operations. Funded by the Mayor’s Office of Housing and Community Development (MOHCD), the team is also part of the San Francisco Digital Services Team. Candidates must be ready to quickly get to work within a multi-disciplinary team. You’ll help improve the affordable housing application product, expand MOHCD’s digital services to meet additional needs of housing applicants, and also collaborate closely with the program team to rethink all service channels in order to streamline the applicant experience.
