Take part in our research studies to improve City services.

  1. Sign up below to get notified when we have research studies.

About our research studies

As we design and build city services, the Digital Services team wants to hear from the public. Through user interviews and user testing, we talk to people who live or work in San Francisco to get their valuable feedback and input. As a participant, you might tell us about your experiences, give input on our designs, or test out a new service we’re working on.

The Digital Services Public Participation program started as Public Voice San Francisco, a collaborative effort by:
